# NoMoreShorts LinkedIn Post Package

## Recommended Post

Use `linkedin-post-copy.md` as the caption.

Attach either:

- `nomoreshorts-linkedin-hero-1080x1350.png` as a single-image post, or
- `nomoreshorts-linkedin-carousel.pdf` as a document carousel.

The carousel source PNGs are also included if you want to post individual images.

## Research Notes

The post uses:

- A direct first-line hook.
- A short personal story.
- Specific before/after context.
- Short paragraphs for scanning.
- A concrete closing question to invite comments.
- Relevant hashtags.

LinkedIn's own sharing guide recommends asking questions, sharing a point of view, using relevant hashtags, responding to commenters, and including images or rich media to draw people in.

For image sizing, the portrait hero is 1080x1350. The carousel slides are 1080x1080 and exported as a PDF for document-style posting.
